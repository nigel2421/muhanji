Dr. Stella Isendi Muhanji

Associate Professor of Economics, Laikipia University, Kenya

Dr. Muhanji is an accomplished Associate Professor of Economics at Laikipia University. Her research primarily focuses on **financial economics, external debt mitigation and management issues, economics of climate change, and monetary policy.**

Research Interests & Projects

My research interests encompass critical areas within economics, aiming to contribute to policy-making and academic discourse. I am particularly focused on:

  • **Financial Economics:** Exploring market dynamics, financial instruments, and their impact on economic stability.
  • **External Debt Mitigation and Management Issues:** Investigating strategies for sustainable debt management in developing economies, with a focus on Africa.
  • **Economics of Climate Change:** Analyzing the economic implications of climate change and exploring policy responses for adaptation and mitigation.
  • **Monetary Policy:** Examining the role and effectiveness of monetary policy in achieving macroeconomic objectives.

My doctoral thesis, "Mitigating the Effects of External Debt Burden in Africa," exemplifies my dedication to addressing pertinent economic challenges facing the continent.
